Boksburg Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Lindiwe Mokoena

WDD 230

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Boksburg Chamber of Commerce is to promote local, national and international commerce in the city of Boksburg. This purpose will be catalized by letting local businesses advertise in the website and it will also serve as a billboard for local commercial events.

Scenarios

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

What educational opportunities the chamber has for the members?

What has been the population growth in the area?

How can I enroll my business in the chamber?
